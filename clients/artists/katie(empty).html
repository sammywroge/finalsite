Katie Lynne Sharbaugh

Singer/Songwriter

Katie Lynne Sharbaugh is a 23-year-old singer-songwriter based in Nashville, Tennessee. Since graduating from Berklee College of Music in 2021, she has released a debut double EP entitled “Little Blue Beetle” and plans to release a full-length album as her next project. Her style combines elements of jazz, pop, and musical theater, featuring cinematic instrumentals and melodic storytelling. In 2022, Katie’s original “Tornado Warnings” gained attention on Tik Tok and was covered by Joe Jonas in a viral duet. “Tornado Warnings” was released as a single later that year and will appear on her upcoming project, “Upstaged.”
