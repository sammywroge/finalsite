Sam Alesandri

Singer/Songwriter

Sam Alesandri is a 20-year-old singer-songwriter from Ohio. Never took a voice lesson and self taught herself how to play guitar, she began her music career as a 10-year-old, where she would sing to small crowds at karaoke night. Since then, she has released two singles, one of which was a cover of Eminem's "Lose Yourself", that went viral on TikTok and Instagram.
