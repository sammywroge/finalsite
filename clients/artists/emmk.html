Emm K

Singer/Songwriter

Emm K is a singer/songwriter located in the Eau Claire and Chippewa Falls area. Her unique sound of alternative, acoustic indie, punk, or a little bit of everything is something that makes her appealing to all audiences. In the past, she has opened for the metal band, Strange Daze, and released her debut EP, "Hometown Stockholm Syndrome", after selling out her first headline show.
