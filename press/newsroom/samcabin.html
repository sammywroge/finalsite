Sam Alesandri opens up for Carol Ades at The Cabin

February 11th, 2023

Sam Alesandri performs at The Cabin at the University of Wisconsin — Eau Claire (UWEC) for what is already her second time since September.

After seeing Lizzy McAlpine — an artist Sam looks up to — and Carol Ades perform in Minneapolis, MN on the "Five Seconds Flat" tour back in September, Sam quickly became a fan of Carol Ades.

“What if I opened for Carol Ades one day?”,

said Sam Alesandri at the Lizzy McAlpine concert.

Now, worlds collide as Sam drove all the way from Ohio to Eau Claire, WI to open up for Carol Ades this past Saturday, February 11th.
