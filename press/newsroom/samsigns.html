Sam Alesandri Signs with good company creations

December 21st, 2022

Singer/Songwriter, Sam Alesandri — based in Ohio — is the second musician to sign with good company creations.

Sam began her music career singing at karaoke nights at the age of 10 and has since released two singles on all streaming platforms. One of which is a cover of Eminem's, "Lose Yourself" that went viral on both TikTok and Instagram.

With new projects and releases in the works, stay tuned for more music coming soon!

Follow Sam Alesandri on Spotify and Apple Music!
