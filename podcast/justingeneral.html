Just In General

Sami Geiger, Samara Sims, Abby Smith, Rebekka Vold, and Sammy Wroge

Just In General is a bi-weekly podcast focusing on a different broad topic each episode. Sami, Samara, Abby, Rebekka, and Sammy come together to debate their takes on common topics and issues. Hilarious rambles, rants, jokes, and stories from the minds of five confused and creative girls who make it through each episode one cackle at a time.
